About me

Simeon Taskaris

GIS Developer from Thessaloniki

I am an Urban Planner with a passion for GIS. I specialize in developing WebGIS applications and Desktop GIS solutions using the ArcGIS products. I am also interested in the development of mobile GIS applications for Android. Additionally, I have a particular focus in the use of open source solutions and geospatial web services orchestration.

I have more than 8 years experience in the field of GIS development. During the last years, I provide consulting services in fields of G.I.S, transportation and intelligent transportation systems. I had been a freelance worker of TRIAS S.A., a Greek Consulting Engineers company in Transport Planning and GIS. Since 2013, I am working as a GIS developer at DRAXIS S.A.
